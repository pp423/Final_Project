Who am I

Hi guys! My name is Piyush Pardeshi, & right now I'm a graduate student at NJIT, Newark. I'm studying Informations Systems here. To my surprise, a decent number of my answers were getting the Best Answer status. I wasn't in the leader board though as I never had that much time to spend on Yahoo Answers. I was working back then. But I kept doing it, whenever I used to get free time. I use to like giving my solution to the people & their problems.

This website came out of a project for my Web Development course. As I'm studying Information Systems I've a few technical subjects of my interest. Apart from giving suggestions to the people (which I consider a habit), my actual hobbies are Camping, Hiking, Listening to Music, Trying every cuisine, listening to music, swimming, fashion & also showcasing my lifestyle.
